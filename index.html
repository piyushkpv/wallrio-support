Frequently Asked Questions

How do I restore my purchase?

Open WallRio → Settings → Restore Purchases.

I purchased Pro but it's still locked.

Tap Restore Purchases or contact support with your Apple ID receipt.

Downloads aren't working.

Check your internet connection and ensure the latest version of WallRio is installed.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Open Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions → WallRio.

I found inappropriate content.

Please contact us with a screenshot and wallpaper title so we can review it immediately.
